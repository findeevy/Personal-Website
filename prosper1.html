some quick notes on my thought process for "videogame" creation:

  • digital interactive art or "videogames" is perhaps the newest mainstream artistic medium humanity has stumbled upon and should be looked at as such by its caretakers.

  • style is substance and vice versa, anyone telling you otherwise is either confusing D.I.A. (digital interactive art) for a slot machine or a textbook.

  • D.I.A. shouldn't be lambasted for having downtime or "slow parts", not everything needs to be a high octane dopamine rush or traditionally fun to be worthwhile. if this wasn't true all humanity would need is tiktok, heroin, and an iv drip.
